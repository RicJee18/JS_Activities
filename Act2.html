My College Life

     Before I tell you my college life story, I am going to introduce myself first. So, I am Jeric Baterna, 20 years old. A Passerelles Numeriques scholar and currently living here in Nasipit, Talamban Cebu City. You can see me at Samjung Building. Besides, I am very proud to be part of USC - Carolinians. First and foremost, I would like to express my deepest gratitude to PN for having this kind of opportunity that can showcase my potentials and expand my learnings technically.

     College life is somewhat like the final stage you need to surpass, the final door rather. After entering this, you can be something different pretty sure. Yes, Success is the product of struggle. Why I am talking here about the struggle it’s because this thing is part of our journey all along. Having IT as a course is not my first choice. I’m not fond of doing this before. Information Technology needs deeper understanding, analysis, logical thinking, problem solving, decision making, and such. It is not that easy and should be judged. At first, I’m so worried about what might happen next. If I can do it or what if I might fail? All I have that time are those queries and negative thoughts randomly beading in my head. While having my first subject turned to first class turned to first semester and turned to first year, it was like living in a world that was full of adjustments, responsibilities, and complex missions.

     In the long run, I’m getting used to it and I am slowly dancing with the flow . I started exploring myself to the life of a programmer and understanding their way of coping stress. Finding errors. Have some trouble shooting and worst is doing codes on your own. Today, I could say that I enjoyed and had some fun with this course. I am having a new friends and a family. And finally realize that being an IT is the life where I am supposed to be.
